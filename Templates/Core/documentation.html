How to begin

You are in the right place to create your professional website We are here to help you create the type of website you want.
You can begin by signing up and choosing from our list of available templates.



Creating account

To create an account after sucessfully launching DSYNE website, you have the options to sign up if you do not have an account.
You can sign up using your facebook, google or apple account.

Another option to creating a new account is by using your email.


  • Enter your email address in the “add email here” field.
  • Enter your preferred password in the password field.
  • Confirm your password and make sure it is same as the one in the password field and click on sign up.

A notification will be sent to your registered email address. You can now log in to your account.



How to open an existing project

To open an existing project in DSYNE. Either:

  • Log in to your registered account
  • Navigate to your dashnoard
  • If you have an existing project, you will be able to view them at the top row of your screen

Changing account password

There are two ways users can change password on DSYNE Platform

  • Forgot Password
    • For users in this category, locate the forgot passoword button on the Log in Page and click to be redirected to a forgot password page
    • Input the email you used in opening your account and click on Reset Password button to get a link via your email
    • Clikc the link sent to your email to set a new password
  • Periodic change of password
    • Users who wish to change their password can do this by going to their profile settings
    • Click on change Password
    • Enter your old password
    • Enter your new passsword and save.
